FNB - Licence Renewals

Case study


Design process

1. Empathy: Understanding the user's needs.

2. Define: Identifying the problem.

3. Ideate: Exploring various possible solutions.

4. Prototype: Creating low-scale representations of solutions.

5. Test: Testing each solution and refine.

6. Implement: Implementation of the solution in real-life.

Objective

To simplify and streamline the vehicle licence renewal process for users by offering a convenient, secure, and efficient platform to handle all aspects of the renewal, including document submission, payment, and notifications, without the need to visit government offices or stand in long lines.

Competitor analyses

To better understand the landscape and identify opportunities for innovation, I conducted a thorough competitor analysis focusing on user experience, interface design, and feature sets. This process helped uncover both industry standards and gaps that could be leveraged to differentiate our product.
