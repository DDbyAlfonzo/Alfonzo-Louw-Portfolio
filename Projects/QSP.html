Quick Score Pro

Case study


Design process

1. Empathy: Understanding the user's needs.

2. Define: Identifying the problem.

3. Ideate: Exploring various possible solutions.

4. Prototype: Creating low-scale representations of solutions.

5. Test: Testing each solution and refine.

6. Implement: Implementation of the solution in real-life.

Objective

Club owners rely on outdated manual processes to run their clubs, such as organising matches, assigning referees, and handling participant registrations and payments. Referees also face significant challenges, often using paper-based systems to record scores. These are not only prone to human error but can also be easily tampered with, lost, or damaged.

The objective was to digitise the process and introduce cutting-edge features designed to streamline match bookings and enhance match management efficiency. By leveraging modern technology, the solution aimed to reduce administrative overhead, accelerate scheduling, and improve the overall user experience for both players and organizers.
