Vikela Tech Portal

Case study


Design process

1. Empathy: Understanding the user's needs.
2. Define: Identifying the problem.
3. Ideate: Exploring various possible solutions.
4. Prototype: Creating low-scale representations of solutions.
5. Test: Testing each solution and refine.
6. Implement: Implementation of the solution in real-life.

Objective

Users required a centralised interface to manage their smart safe operations efficiently. This included the ability to customize settings, monitor activity in real-time, add or remove connected devices, print transaction receipts, and accurately track and count deposited bills.

What have I learned

Collaboration is key: Working closely with developers, product owners, and QA early reduced handover issues and surprises later.
Design is a conversation: The best ideas often emerged through open feedback sessions and team brainstorming.
Clear documentation: Creating annotated wireframes and specs helped ensure design intent carried through to implementation..
Accessibility: Designing for screen readers, color contrast, and keyboard navigation improved usability for all users
